Composite shapes

Posted on April 5, 2025

This is the first mandala I've made that is using the new 'composite shapes'. Composite shapes are shapes that are made up of multiple other shapes. If you try out the interactions on this one - hover or click or swipe on part of it - you'll see that these dotted arcs are treated as one shape. This will let us create more complex and interesting shapes without having to recreate each of the parts individually.
