About MandalaVis

I am planning to use this space to explore some data visualization techniques inspired by mandalas. My background is a combination of software development and biomedical engineering. My top two interests are

  1. current research on the human gut microbiome and it's impact on overall health and
  2. developing beautiful mandala inspired data visualizations.
I will combine these two things so I'll post some of the latest microbiome research and I'll make some data visualizations with the goal of clarifying the data.
